CONCEPT-001
keywords: transport poverty, definition, mobility disadvantage, accessibility
questions:
- What does transport poverty mean in this project?
- What is transport poverty?
- 交通贫困是什么意思？
- 这个项目里的 transport poverty 代表什么？
answer_zh:
在这个项目里，transport poverty 不是只看有没有公交。它指的是居民因为公共交通不足、基本服务可达性差、社会脆弱性高，或者空间结构不利，而更难到达日常机会和服务。它是一个综合诊断概念，不是单一变量。
answer_en:
In this project, transport poverty is not only about whether public transport exists. It means that residents may face weak access to daily opportunities because of poor public transport, weak essential service access, social vulnerability, or territorial disadvantage. It is a combined diagnostic concept, not one single variable.

answer_en:
All final scores are aligned in the concern direction. 0 means low concern, and 1 or 100 means high concern. So in the final layers, a higher value means a more serious issue or a stronger need for attention.

CONCEPT-003
keywords: accessibility, deficit, inverse, conversion
questions:
- Why do you convert accessibility into deficit?
- What is the difference between accessibility and deficit?
- 为什么 accessibility 要转成 deficit？
- deficit 是怎么来的？
answer_zh:
Accessibility 表示可达性，值越高越好。Deficit 表示不足，值越高问题越严重。为了让所有图层方向一致，我把可达性变量转换成 deficit。基本公式是 Deficit = 1 - Accessibility。
answer_en:
Accessibility means how easy it is to reach something, so higher is better. Deficit means lack of access, so higher is worse. To align all final layers in the same direction, accessibility is converted into deficit. The basic formula is Deficit = 1 - Accessibility.

CONCEPT-004

questions:

- What does H3 mean here?

answer_zh:
H3 是 WebGIS 里的统一空间单元。它把不同来源的数据统一到同一种六边形网格里。这样可以比较不同图层，也方便网页快速显示。项目最终主要使用 H3 resolution 9。
answer_en:
H3 is the common spatial unit used in the WebGIS. It converts different data sources into the same hexagonal grid. This makes layers comparable and efficient to display online. The final WebGIS mainly uses H3 resolution 9.

CONCEPT-005
keywords: H3 resolution 9, 100m, spatial support, scale
questions:
- Is H3 resolution 9 the same as 100 metres?
- What is the resolution of the map?
- H3 9 是不是 100 米？
- 地图分辨率是多少？
answer_zh:
H3 resolution 9 不是严格的 100 米方格。它是六边形网格，平均面积大约 0.105 平方公里，平均边长约 200 米。项目里很多计算先在细网格或 100 m 支持上做，最后聚合到 H3 9 用于 WebGIS 展示。
answer_en:
H3 resolution 9 is not exactly a 100 m square grid. It is a hexagonal grid, with an average area of about 0.105 km2 and an average edge length of about 200 m. Many computations are done on fine-grid or 100 m support first, then aggregated to H3 9 for WebGIS display.

SVI means Social Vulnerability Index. It is not simply poverty. It combines elderly population, labour vulnerability, education, citizenship or migrant-related variables, income, and low car access. It identifies groups more exposed to transport and service gaps.

answer_zh:
Stop access 表示居民是否容易到达普通公共交通站点。它主要反映基础覆盖，也就是附近有没有可步行到达的公交、tram、metro 或 rail stop。它更关注 first-mile access。

CONCEPT-012
keywords: Hub access, interchange, connectivity, HA
questions:
- What is hub access?
- What does HA mean?
- Hub access 是什么？
- HA 和 stop access 有什么区别？
answer_zh:
Hub access 表示居民是否容易到达重要交通枢纽或换乘节点。它不是看任意一个普通站点，而是看能否接近更有网络价值的节点，比如 rail、metro、主要换乘站、多线路交汇点或 relevant hub。它更关注网络连接质量。
answer_en:
Hub access measures whether residents can reach important hubs or interchange nodes. It is not about any regular stop. It focuses on access to nodes with stronger network value, such as rail, metro, major interchanges, multi-line stops, or relevant hubs. It captures network connection quality.

CONCEPT-013
keywords: Stop access vs Hub access, difference, SA, HA
questions:
- What is the difference between stop access and hub access?
- Why do you separate stop access and hub access?
- Stop access 和 hub access 有什么区别？
- 为什么 SA 和 HA 要分开？
answer_zh:
Stop access 看基础站点覆盖。Hub access 看重要换乘点或高连接节点。一个地方可能离普通 bus stop 很近，所以 stop access 不错；但它可能离 metro、rail 或主要换乘点很远，所以 hub access 很弱。两者分开可以解释不同类型的公共交通不足。
answer_en:
Stop access captures basic stop coverage. Hub access captures access to important interchange or high-connectivity nodes. A place can be close to a local bus stop and have good stop access, but still be far from metro, rail, or major interchanges and have weak hub access. Separating them helps explain different types of public transport deficit.

CONCEPT-014
keywords: Service frequency, FR, headway, waiting time
questions:
- What does service frequency mean?
- What does FR measure?
- service frequency 是什么？
- FR 代表什么？
answer_zh:
Service frequency 表示公共交通服务有多频繁。频率越高，平均等待时间通常越短，公共交通可用性越强。它补充了 stop access，因为有站点不代表服务频率足够。
answer_en:
Service frequency measures how often public transport services run. Higher frequency usually means shorter waiting time and better usability. It complements stop access because having a stop nearby does not mean service is frequent.

CONCEPT-016
keywords: PTAL, Accessibility Index, AI, EDF
questions:
- What is PTAL?
- How should PTAL be understood?
- PTAL 是什么？
- PTAL 怎么理解？
answer_zh:
PTAL 是 Public Transport Accessibility Level。它基于可步行到达的公共交通服务点、步行时间、等待时间、服务频率和线路权重，计算一个 Accessibility Index，然后分级。它反映公共交通服务可达性，但不等于全天候真实出行体验。
answer_en:
PTAL means Public Transport Accessibility Level. It uses reachable public transport service access points, walking time, waiting time, service frequency, and route weighting to calculate an Accessibility Index and classify it. It reflects public transport accessibility, but it is not the same as real all-day travel experience.

CONCEPT-017
keywords: PTOL, opportunity level, modes, frequency-free
questions:
- What is PTOL?
- How is PTOL different from PTAL?
- PTOL 是什么？
- PTOL 和 PTAL 有什么区别？
answer_zh:
PTOL 是 Public Transport Opportunity Level。它更像一个 frequency-free 的机会指标，主要看一个地方能步行到几种公共交通模式，比如 bus、tram、metro、rail。PTAL 更关注频率、等待和 access time；PTOL 更关注模式选择和机会数量。
answer_en:
PTOL means Public Transport Opportunity Level. It is more like a frequency-free opportunity score. It counts how many public transport modes are walkable, such as bus, tram, metro, and rail. PTAL focuses more on frequency, waiting time, and access time; PTOL focuses more on modal opportunity.

- How does NeTEx supplement GTFS?

GTFS 是主要公共交通数据源，提供站点、线路、班次和频率。NeTEx 用来补充 GTFS 不完整的部分，尤其是区域公共交通服务。它帮助补充 stops、service access points、线路、模式覆盖和区域服务信息。
answer_en:

It helps add stops, service access points, route or line information, mode coverage, and regional service information.

questions:
- What are the limitations of GTFS?
- Does GTFS show real-time service?
- GTFS 有什么限制？
- GTFS 能不能代表实时公交？
answer_zh:
GTFS 主要表示计划服务。它不能完整表示实时延误、取消班次、拥挤、车辆可靠性或突发服务变化。所以它适合做计划服务供给分析，但不能完全代表真实出行体验。
answer_en:
GTFS mainly represents scheduled service. It does not fully capture real-time delays, cancelled trips, crowding, reliability, or temporary disruptions. It is useful for scheduled service supply analysis, but it does not fully represent real travel experience.

CONCEPT-021
keywords: Essential service accessibility, radius, distance, proximity
questions:
- Is essential service accessibility just a radius check?
- Is it only checking if a service exists nearby?
- Essential service accessibility 是不是只看半径内有没有服务？
- 基本服务可达性是不是 yes/no？
answer_zh:
不是简单的 yes/no 半径判断。它会分别计算医疗、学校、就业机会和 grocery 的可达性，主要基于距离或 proximity 的服务可达性。然后把四类服务分数归一化并加权合成 ESA。当前实现仍有局限，例如没有完整建模容量、营业时间、资格限制、拥挤或真实 transit travel time。
answer_en:
It is not only a simple yes/no radius check. Accessibility is computed separately for healthcare, schools, employment opportunities, and grocery access, mainly through distance or proximity-based access. The four category scores are normalised and weighted into ESA. The current implementation still has limitations: it does not fully model capacity, opening hours, eligibility, congestion, or real transit travel time.

answer_zh:
VSEG 表示 vulnerable population 和 essential service deficit 的叠加。公式是 VSEG = SVI × ESD。它不是替代 ESD，而是帮助识别社会脆弱性高且基本服务不足也高的地方。
answer_en:
VSEG means the overlap between vulnerable population and essential service deficit. The formula is VSEG = SVI × ESD. It does not replace ESD. It helps identify places where high social vulnerability overlaps with weak essential service access.
